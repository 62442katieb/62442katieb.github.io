I am a developmental cognitive neuroscientist studying environmental and endocrine influences on the brain as an Assistant Professor at Florida International University. My work focuses on developing and applying rigorous statistical and machine learning methodologies to study influences of hormones and pollutions on brain structure, function, and health mainly during adolescence, but extending soon to look across the lifespan.
